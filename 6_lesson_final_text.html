Valorant is a free-to-play, team-based, tactical first-person shooter developed and published by Riot Games. The game pits two teams of five players against each other, with one team attacking and the other defending, in a series of rounds that culminate in a victory for the first team to reach 13 rounds. Players choose from a roster of unique "Agents," each with distinct abilities that influence gunplay and strategic plays.
